Revolve won the HNU-intern GamesKickstart 2023/24
Revolve Showcase

Revolve was made as a semester project. The task was to choose a video game and adapt its core mechanics into a board game, producing a prototype that included a ruleset, props, and artwork.
I was mainly responsible for the ruleset, building prototypes, leading playtests, and collecting feedback to improve gameplay. As the saying goes, "To learn how to make video games, you first need to learn how to make games."
Over the course of a few months, we built numerous prototypes and conducted countless playtests. As part of a highly motivated group, we managed to win the HNU-internal Games Kickstart competition.
Revolve is based on the extraction shooter Hunt: Showdown. This genre is greatly underrepresented in board games, so we aimed to fill that gap. We even received feedback from Daniel Greiner, a professional game designer at Ravensburger.
